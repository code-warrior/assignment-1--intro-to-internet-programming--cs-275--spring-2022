DIETER RAMS' TEN PRINCIPLES FOR GOOD DESIGN

Who is Dieter Ram?

Dieter Ram was born in Germany May 20th, in the year 1932. He is an industrial designer, who worked closely with the company Braun. A company well known for a variation of consumer products. Yet, this is not what Ram is most known for. Rather, Ram wrote the ten principles of good design. The golden rules in which, a successful deisgn should follow.
